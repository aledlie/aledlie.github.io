KEY INSIGHTS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Minimalist Design Principle
   Your site's strength is CONSISTENCY. Breaking it (even in 7% of content)
   undermines your entire design philosophy.

2. User Expectations
   Users mentally group similar items. When 2 reports look different,
   they assume those reports are lower quality or incomplete.

3. SEO Impact
   Missing excerpts = lost search visibility. Each report should appear
   in Google with a compelling preview that drives clicks.

4. Navigation Clarity
   Breadcrumbs aren't decorative—they're functional. They answer:
   "Where am I?" and "How do I get back?"

5. Professional Appearance
   Headers serve two purposes: (1) Visual interest, (2) Completeness.
   Missing headers make pages look unfinished.
