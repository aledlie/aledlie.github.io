## Schema Types Implemented

The structured data implementation includes 7 schema types:

1. **LocalBusiness** - Core business information with address, geo coordinates, hours
2. **EducationalOrganization** - Dance instruction credentials and programs
3. **Event** (x2) - Dance class schedules and workshop events
4. **VideoObject** (x3) - Tutorial videos with duration, thumbnails, and educational metadata

### Key Schema Properties

- Business name and alternative names
- Physical address with geo coordinates
- Opening hours and price range
- Aggregate ratings and individual reviews
- Course offerings with belt progression system
- Service catalog (wedding dance, birthday parties, quincea√±era)
- Social media links (Facebook, Instagram)
